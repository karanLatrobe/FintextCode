How Fintext Works

Fintext follows an AI-driven processing workflow designed to extract, categorize, and summarize financial data with precision.

1

PDF Data Extraction

The system intelligently reads and scans uploaded bank statements, extracting key transaction details such as date, description, amount, and balance with high accuracy.

2

Smart Categorization

All extracted transactions are automatically analyzed and classified into relevant categories - such as Food, Travel, Bills, Shopping, Salary, and Transfers - using machine-learning models and rule-based logic.

3

Financial Summary

Fintext generates a clear and structured financial summary that highlights total income, expenses, category-wise distributions, and other key insights for seamless financial understanding.
